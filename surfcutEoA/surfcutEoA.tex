\textsc{INPUT}: An undirected graph $G = (V, E)$ embedded on a surface of genus $g$, a non-negative capacity function $c\colon E \to \mathbb{R}$, and two vertices $s$ and $t$.
\textsc{OUTPUT}: A minimum-capacity $(s,t)$-cut in $G$.}
